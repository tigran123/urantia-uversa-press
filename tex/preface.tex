\Large\textsc{Publisher's Preface}
\end{center}

Uversa Press is pleased to bring you this revised 3\ts{rd} edition of \emph{The Urantia Book,} incorporating updated formatting, a universal reference system and an extensive topical index. The text of this edition represents the Standard Reference Text (SRT) of \emph{The Urantia Book.}

\preftitle{THE REFERENCE SYSTEM}

\emph{The Urantia Book} consists of a Foreword and 196 numbered Papers.
Each Paper is divided into numbered sections, and each section is divided into numbered paragraphs.
The reference system locates a passage using three numbers to identify its Paper, section, and paragraph.
For example, 185:3.5 refers to Paper 185, section 3, paragraph 5.
The Foreword is numbered as Paper 0; likewise, the introductory statement at the beginning of each Paper is numbered as section 0.
The locators printed at the top outside page margin indicate the first full paragraph and the last paragraph on the page.

\preftitle{THE INDEX}

The index in this volume consists of approximately 40,000 entries and is used by permission of the author.
Please note that the abbreviation “q.v.” after a word or phrase means one should look under that word or phrase for the reference sought, and that the abbreviation “cf:” means “compare,” in the sense that the reference makes an interesting but distinct point.

\preftitle{THE URANTIA BOOK FELLOWSHIP}

The Urantia Book Fellowship, a non-profit education and service organization founded in 1955, is the original Urantia Book reader membership organization.
If you find yourself intrigued by \emph{The Urantia Book,} please feel free to contact us to add your name to our mailing list, to contact other readers in your area, or to learn about Urantia Book-related gatherings and conferences.

On the Internet, \myurl{urantiabook.org} provides extensive information about \emph{The Urantia Book} and related subjects and activities.

Our address and telephone number are listed on the opposite page.
We warmly welcome your interest.

\preftitle{THE FOOTNOTES}

The 1955 first printing of \emph{The Urantia Book} contained a small number of minor typographical errors which have been discovered by readers over the years.
Therefore, the two publishers of \emph{The Urantia Book,} The Urantia Book Fellowship and Urantia Foundation, together created a joint committee to examine these issues and to make recommendations for resolving each of them.
Both publishers approved the final decisions of the committee and both have adopted the SRT for for all printed and electronic editions of \emph{The Urantia Book.}
In this edition, at every point where the SRT differs from the 1955 edition of \emph{The Urantia Book,} the text as found in the latter is printed in the footnote at the bottom of the page.
Details of the reasoning behind these changes as well as general information about the SRT are available online at: \myurl{urantiabook.org/SRT}
